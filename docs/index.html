The Human "Boil-Up": didbox402 isn't just a database. It's a vending machine for privacy. Agents pay for exactly what they use, for exactly how long they need it, and they prove who they are with DIDs.

The Mainnet Bridge: v0.6.2 implemented real L402 + x402 settlement. v0.7.0 adds Sovereign Mobility (client-driven migration with verifiable Migration Proofs) and significantly expands the Conformance Suite for real payment rails.
